Navigation
Bottom tab bar
NewBottom navigation bar
Frosted glass tab bar with Calculator, Calendar, and Account tabs. Appears on all main screens including the Get Started splash screen. Active tab highlights in purple. Hides automatically when bottom sheets open.
NewAccount screen
Shows your email address, a New Calculation shortcut, and a Sign Out button with confirmation prompt.
📅
Calendar
WeekCal-style month view
NewMini month grid with inline job bars
Full WeekCal-style calendar grid. Multi-day jobs render as continuous colour bars spanning across days. The job title is centred over its span on every row — no more truncation on week breaks. Up to 3 job lanes per row with overflow indicator.
NewAdd / Edit Shoot sheet
Bottom sheet with production name, start/end dates, role dropdown (all ACCP roles with auto-filled day rates), pay, 10 colour swatches, and notes. Role selection auto-fills the day rate field.
NewSub-event day types with gradient bars
Each job can have Pre-light , Weather Hold , Prep , and Wrap days. Prep and Wrap are date ranges (multi-day). Pre-light and Weather Hold are individual dates with "+ Add another" support. Special days show as gradient colour blends on the bar — same job, different day colour. The job's date range automatically extends to include all sub-event dates.
NewDelete with confirmation
Trash icon on job cards opens a confirmation sheet naming the job before deleting. No accidental deletes.
NewShare job link
Share icon on cards opens a sheet with two options: Share Full Job (title, dates, role, pay, colour encoded in URL) or Share Hours Only (title and dates only, no pay info). On iOS uses native share sheet; on desktop copies link to clipboard. Recipients opening the link see a confirmation prompt and the job is added directly to their calendar.
NewCalculate Hours from calendar job
Each job card has a Calc button that launches the calculator pre-configured for that job. Prep/wrap days auto-toggle the correct day type. Saved calculations restore times and results. A Save to Job button writes results back to the calendar entry. Shows "Saved" badge when calc data exists.
ImprovedPast events auto-grey
Jobs whose end date has passed automatically render with desaturated grey bars and reduced opacity in both the grid and detail cards.
🧮
Calculator
ACCP billing engine
NewPrep / Wrap day toggle per day
Each day panel has a Prep/Wrap day toggle. When enabled, Prep and Wrap type buttons appear — the first day defaults to Prep, the last day to Wrap. Rate auto-fills from the role's prep/wrap rate (Stylist $751, Asst Stylist/MU $502). Prep/Wrap days use a flat 10H rate with no OT or meal penalties.
ImprovedStyling & Make Up roles simplified
Separate "(prep & wrap)" role entries removed. Stylist, Assistant Stylist, and Assistant Make Up are now single entries. Prep/wrap rate is baked into each role and auto-fills when the day toggle is turned on.
NewSave to Calendar from results
New "Save to Calendar" button in the results screen. Opens a sheet to confirm production name, dates, and colour, then saves the job with full calc data to the calendar and jumps straight to it.
ImprovedPrep/Wrap badge in results
Day result cards show a purple "prep" or green "wrap" badge on days with those toggles set, making multi-day breakdowns easy to scan.
📋
Timesheet
Preview & export
NewExport Excel button
Exports a full .xlsx workbook via SheetJS (loaded on demand). Includes all day rows with times and OT, totals, reimbursements, meal penalty details, and turnaround info. Each crew member gets their own sheet tab in batch mode.
NewMeal penalties and turnaround in preview
A notes section below the earnings row now shows each meal penalty alert with day number and dollar amount, and each day-pair turnaround with rest hours and any shortfall penalty. Appears in both HTML preview and PDF/Excel exports.
FixedMeal Pen column now shows dollar amount
The Meal Pen column in the timesheet table was showing blank. Now correctly displays the penalty dollar amount (e.g. $62.69) in the HTML preview, PDF export, and Excel export.
NewPrivacy notice on sensitive fields
A green "Never stored or transmitted — for your PDF only" notice appears below the SIN # and HST/GST number fields in both single and batch modes.
NewRole dropdown in calendar Add Shoot sheet
The Role field is now a dropdown matching the full ACCP role list, grouped by department. Selecting a role auto-fills the Day rate field (flat roles fill the day rate; hourly roles fill rate × 10h).

Bug Fixes
Stability & layout
FixedCalendar and Save to Calendar sheets blocked by nav bar
Sheet z-index raised above the nav bar (1100 vs 1000). Nav bar also hides when any sheet opens and reappears when it closes. Cancel/Save buttons now always reachable above the home indicator.
FixedAdd Shoot button overlapping nav bar
Floating Add Shoot button repositioned 72px above the window bottom (above the nav bar). Calendar screen now has 130px bottom padding so job detail cards scroll fully clear of the button.
FixedCalendar bar labels truncating
Labels now show on the first cell of every row the job spans (not just the very first day), centred over that row's visible portion. Bar shapes also fixed to round correctly at week breaks.
FixedGet Started screen unresponsive
A literal newline inside a JS string in the calendar import handler was crashing the entire script block on load. Fixed by escaping as \n. All code passes Node.js syntax validation.
FixedSafe area padding across all screens
Calendar header, role picker, invoice, account, and add-shoot sheet all respect env(safe-area-inset-top) and env(safe-area-inset-bottom). Back buttons and action buttons no longer hide behind the Dynamic Island or home indicator.
FixedDelete button onclick syntax error
The trash button on calendar job cards had a broken onclick attribute with empty string concatenation. Fixed using HTML-encoded double quotes (&quot;) for the job ID argument.
FixedTurnaround data missing from timesheet
Turnaround info was only set in liveUpdatePreview (fires as you type) but not in generateSingle or generateBatch. Fixed across all three sheet-building paths plus buildTSHtml itself.
FixedCorrupted double-document file
A failed function replacement resulted in a 5200-line double-document where the JS parser hit HTML mid-script. Reconstructed from the two clean sections back to a single 3300-line file.
